About Swastik Transport

Leading the logistics industry with innovation, reliability, and excellence since 1995

Our Story

Founded on Excellence

Established in 1995, Swastik Transport Pvt. Ltd. has grown from a small local transport company to one of India's leading logistics providers. Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide reliable, efficient, and cost-effective transport solutions.

Today, we operate a fleet of over 500 vehicles and serve more than 10,000 customers across the country. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner in the logistics industry.

Our Mission

To provide world-class logistics solutions that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety, reliability, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative logistics partner in India, connecting businesses and communities through seamless transport solutions.
